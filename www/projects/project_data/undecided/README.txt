Full data format, data types:

Savings rate
	- in %
	- Ratio of savings to nominal GDP
	- For each country
	- Relevant year from 2000 to 2018
	- Source:

Unemployment rate
	- in %
	- Ratio of unemployed population to labor force
	- For each country
	- Relevant year from 2000 to 2018
	- Source:

Male-to-population ratio
	- in %
	- Ratio of male population to the overall population
	- For each country
	- Relevant year from 2000 to 2018
	- Source:

Female-to-population ratio
	- in %
	- Ratio of female population to the overall population
	- For each country
	- Relevant year from 2000 to 2018
	- Source:

Total population
	- Number of people
	- For each country
	- Relevant year from 2000 to 2018
	- Source: https://data.worldbank.org/indicator/SP.POP.TOTL
	- Last updated: 2020-02-27

Population density
	- Number of people per square kilometres of land area
	- For each country
	- Relevant year from 2000 to 2018
	- Source: https://data.worldbank.org/indicator/EN.POP.DNST
	- Last updated: 2020-02-27

Nominal GDP
	- in current local currency units relative to the U.S. dollar
	- For each country
	- Relevant year from 2000 to 2018
	- Source: https://data.worldbank.org/indicator/NY.GDP.MKTP.CN
	- Last updated: 2020-02-27

Nominal GDP per capita
	- in current local currency units relative to the U.S. dollar
	- For each country
	- Relevant year from 2000 to 2018
	- Source: https://data.worldbank.org/indicator/NY.GDP.PCAP.CN
	- Last updated: 2020-02-27

GDP growth
	- in %
	- For each country
	- Relevant year from 2000 to 2018
	- Source: https://data.worldbank.org/indicator/NY.GDP.MKTP.KD.ZG
	- Last updated: 2020-02-27

Age dependency ratio, working age
	- in %
	- For each country
	- Relevant year from 2000 to 2018
	- Source: World Development Indicators
	- Last update: 2020-02-27

Age dependency ratio, young
	- in %
	- For each country
	- Relevant year from 2000 to 2018
	- Source: World Development Indicators
	- Last update: 2020-02-27

Literacy rate
	- in % of population ages 15 and above
	- For each country
	- Relevant year from 2000 to 2018
	- Source: World Development Indicators
	- Last update: 2020-02-27

Education
	- in % of population 25 and above
	- Educational attainment of Doctoral or equivalent
	- For each country
	- Relevant year from 2000 to 2018
	- Source: World Development Indicators
	- Last update: 2020-02-27
